This is a maracuya…

…my favorite Colombian fruit.

But Maracuya IT is my company for giving classes about R. I offer four cources at the moment:

  1. R - Basics, for people who have zero or little experience with R. The basic about how to explore data, create graphics, do some simple statiscical models and present the results is explained.
  2. R - Machine Learning, this course focusses on using more complex statistical models in R. Randomforrest, clustering and cross validation are explained.
  3. R - Big Data, here I explain how to connect R to a cluster and do computations on large amounts of data using sparklyr.
  4. R - Shiny, explains how to make interactive website and dashboards with R using shiny.

This website will contain examples of what you can do with the knowledge optained in these courses.
