Colombian Cooking

May 23, 2018 in R , SHINY
cooking randommonkey
1 min read

Last December I had the opportunity to work with my friends from randommonkey. We made special about the Colombian kitchen. Part of this special was a Shiny application that can be used to search trough the Colombian recipes. Names, ingredient, region, complexity and the time required can be used to find your favorite one. After finding a recipe you can download the description.

So if you are interested in making some Mojarras fritas (and you know a little Spanish because the page is completely in Spanish) look here.
